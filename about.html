About Leano

Your Strategic Security Partner

We don't just protect — you thrive under our shield

At Leano Security Services, we've evolved from voluntary operations in 2017 to a fully registered security powerhouse. Founded on the principles of accountability, excellence, and integrity, our commitment is to redefine security standards through innovation, people empowerment, and an unwavering focus on customer service.

We are not just a security company — we are your strategic partner in safety and risk management. Every day, we work tirelessly to ensure that your assets, your people, and your peace of mind are protected by the best in the business.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide security services that go beyond the ordinary. Today, we stand as a testament to what dedication, innovation, and a client-first approach can achieve.
