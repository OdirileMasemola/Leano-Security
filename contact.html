Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our security services

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

Our average emergency response time is under 3 minutes in urban areas. We maintain strategically positioned response teams and use advanced GPS tracking to ensure rapid deployment.

What areas do you service?

We provide comprehensive security services across South Africa, with primary operations in major metropolitan areas. Our services extend to residential, commercial, industrial, and remote locations nationwide.

Are your security officers properly trained and certified?

Yes, all our security personnel are PSIRA registered and undergo rigorous training. We're also NKP accredited, meaning our officers meet the highest national security standards for protecting critical infrastructure.

Can I get a customized security plan for my business?

Absolutely. We offer free security consultations and risk assessments. Our experts will develop a customized security plan tailored to your specific needs, industry requirements, and budget constraints.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and debit orders. We also offer flexible payment plans for long-term contracts to accommodate different budget requirements.

Do you provide 24/7 monitoring services?

Yes, our state-of-the-art control room operates 24/7, 365 days a year. We provide continuous monitoring of alarm systems, CCTV cameras, and GPS tracking with immediate response capabilities.
